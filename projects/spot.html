SPOT Player Obliteration Tactics

A collection of location-based, family friendly games!

Project Description

Our project for the Summer 2024 Focal Point Internship. SPOT is a collection of games that use the players physical location in order to play. The games include King of the Hill, Dead Drop, and Chase the Rabbit. The app requires the use of a separate GPS Logging app to track the player's location.
