Rock Paper Scissors Gun

A classic game with a fun twist!

Project Description

A new twist on a classic game! In addition to rock, paper, and scissors, gun adds some needed chaos. Players are able to play against the CPU, playing best 2 of 3. Players can also see their own lifetime statistics, alongside a global leaderboard.
This site was my project for my Intro to Web Development class. It started as a simple HTML page, and over time I upgraded it with CSS, JavaScript, and eventually converted it to React.
